  ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  2. Menu slides in from left (300ms)
  3. Overlay fades in
  4. Lines transform to X

  Close Menu:
  • Click hamburger again
  • Click overlay backdrop
  • Click any menu link
  • Press ESC key
  • Swipe left on menu
  • Resize to desktop width
