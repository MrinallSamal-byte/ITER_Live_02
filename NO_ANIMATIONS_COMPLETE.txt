TO ADD TO OTHER PAGES:
  1. Add to <head> section:
     <link rel="stylesheet" href="../css/no-animations.css">
     <script src="../js/no-animations.js"></script>
  
  2. Place no-animations.js BEFORE other scripts
  3. Refresh page - animations gone!

TO REVERT (Enable animations again):
  1. Comment out or remove:
     <!-- <link rel="stylesheet" href="../css/no-animations.css"> -->
     <!-- <script src="../js/no-animations.js"></script> -->
  
  2. Refresh page - animations back!
