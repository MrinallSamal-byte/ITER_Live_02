About ITER, SOA University

A Legacy of Excellence in Technical Education Since 1996

The Institute of Technical Education and Research (ITER) is a premier constituent college of Siksha 'O' Anusandhan (Deemed to be University), Bhubaneswar, Odisha. Established with a vision to provide world-class technical education, ITER has been at the forefront of fostering innovation, research, and academic excellence for over two decades.

SOA University is NAAC A++ accredited, recognized by UGC under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956, and has been granted Category-I status. ITER offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various engineering disciplines, all designed to meet industry demands and global standards.
